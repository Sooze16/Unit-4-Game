Snoopy's Snowflake Game

Snoopy's Snowflakes:

Snoopy's snowflakes have a hidden value that will only be revealed when clicked. With each click, you will add the snowflake's value to your total score. You will win the game if your total score matches Snoopys number. If you go over, you get to shovel. Good luck.
